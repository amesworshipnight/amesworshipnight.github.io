# Who We Are
Ames Worship Night gathers once a month to worship Jesus together in Ames, IA. We exist to glorify God, grow as a family, and invite our city into the presence of God.

## Purpose
To lead the congregation in worship to God, creating an atmosphere that encourages a deeper connection with God by providing high-quality music that enhances the worship experience.

## Mission
We lead in heartfelt, Spirit-filled worship, developing our gifts, growing together, and fostering a culture of respect, support, and learning.

## Vision
To create an atmosphere of authentic worship in Ames that invites the presence of God and inspires a lifestyle of worship beyond the church walls.

## Core Values
- Audience of One (Colossians 3:23-24)
- Faith, respect, unity, excellence, honesty
- Passion for worship and love for people
